Electric Renegade Career and Discography

17 July 1969

The Band is formed

The band was formed in 1969, but did not gain any real attention until 1973, when they were booked their first show at a small bar in West Hollywood.

25 May 1975

Signed with BMI

After dropping an album that charted at #10 on the US top albums, Electric Renegade signed with BMI, with a $1 million dollar deal. They made 5 albums under this contract, and these albums are considered to be some of their best work.

08 May 1981

Band Breaks up

The band broke up for eight years while all band members pursued making their own music, with their own unique styles. During the eight years, Danny Poole, the drummer, sadly passed away. He is still considered to be the best drummer of all time.

30 May 1989

The Band Reunites

After the death of Danny Poole, the band members agreed that the best thing they could do was to reunite the band in Danny Poole's memory. The band has been reunited ever since, and has charted many times.
