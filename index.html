Band History

The Electric Brigade is an English Rock Band formed in Oxford in 1969. With members Jimmy Gray, Edwin Lowe, Rex Sutton, and Danny Poole, they became known as one of the most influential bands of all time. Their music was described as hard rock, blues rock, and heavy metal. They paved the way for other bands such as AC/DC, Black Sabbath, Led Zeppelin etc. Electric Brigade sold more than 200 million records worldwide, including 71.5 million albums in the United States. Their album “Electric Shock,” sold more than 45 million units worldwide.
